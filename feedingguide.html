Feeding Guide

Here we will give you a basic guide on how to feed your dog correctly.

Feeding guide

The amount of food your dog needs depends on their size, breed and level of activity. Detailed feeding instructions can be found on the back of most food packs. This will vary depending on the product. The higher quality products will have lower amounts due to the higher nutritional value per gram being higher. As a guide, feed your dog 90g of food for every 5kg of body weight. If your dog is over 32kg, feed them 70g of food for every Skg of body weight.

A complete dry food diet would give the recommendation, of feeding your dog two or more servings per day. It's important not to over feed your dog. This can be easily achieved without being noticed by giving your dog household scraps or treats at your when vou're eating or leaving them alone for a while.

Feeding should be a strict but regular process. Fresh, clean water should also always be available. To help keep their teeth clean, give your dog something hard, such as bone or chews, on a regular basis.
