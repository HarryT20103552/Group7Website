Need Help

Here you will find our most frequently asked questions with answers, if you are still unsure, get in touch with us by filling out the contact form.

FAQ'S:

Q: Are you a dog charity?

A: Whilst we are not actually a dog charity ourselves, we provide all the important information regarding dogs and dog care, and provide you with charitys which you can donate to. See the 'Donations. page for more information

Q: Can I adopt a dog through your site?

A: You cannot adopt a dog directly through our site, as we are not actually a dog adoption site, we are a dog information site. However we do provide you with information on how to adopt, and where to adopt. Please see the 'Adoption' page for more information

Q: What is the purpose of this site?

A: The purpose of our site is to provide new and existing dog owners, or potential dog owners, with all the important information regarding dog care.
